Intro to Soccer

Are you interested in soccer but don't know much about it? Then lucky for you, you've found the perfect place to learn the basics and plant the seed for your love of this wonderful sport. Click through this website to find more information on how to play, how to get involved, and the worldwide impact of soccer.

The Basics

Introduction

Soccer is one of the many sports played by people worldwide. Millions of people play, and players' levels range from recreational to professional. See your local soccer club for information on how to join a team in your area!

How to Play

To play soccer, you'll first need a team. Specific regulations, such as how many players are on the field at once, vary from age level to age level. See the chart below to find out which rules differ, and which ones your team should follow.

Age Level Number of Players Size of Ball Length of Game
U6 to U8 4v4 3 4x10 min
U9 to U10 7v7 4 2x25 min
U11 to U12 9v9 4 2x30 min
U13 and older 11v11 5 check local rules
Beware! These rules are flexible, so make sure to find out if your local soccer club has modified them.

Rules of the Game

As with any other sport, there are plenty of rules to follow in soccer. These rules aim to make gameplay fair, and to keep players safe.The most important thing to know about soccer is that players are not allowed to use their hands to control the ball. The aim of the game is to score a point by getting the soccer ball in the opposing team's goal. To do this, players can use any body part (except for hands, of course!).Visit the International Football Association Board website for the full list of rules.
